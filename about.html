About Me!

My Story

I grew up with restricted time to play video games or watch television, but I had unlimited time to mess around on Scratch, which is where my passion for coding began. I often struggled for ideas, but when I had one, I made it happen. I enjoyed the challenge of every bug fix.

My Traits

I am positive, hard working, smart, creative, and most of all, I love solving problems. I often play escape room style games and get bored when my brain is not challenged for too long, like if I am watching television or playing a game that is too easy. I also love to talk, and many people close to me enjoy having conversations with me.
